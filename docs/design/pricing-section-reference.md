# Pricing Section Reference

This note preserves the pricing section idea from the shared 21st.dev component without importing it directly.

## What To Reuse

- Clear plan comparison cards.
- Monthly price as the main visual anchor.
- Feature bullets with simple confirmation markers.
- A clear current-plan state.
- One primary action per plan.

## What Not To Reuse

- Framer Motion layout shifts.
- Canvas confetti.
- Animated number dependencies.
- Consumer-style "popular" excitement.
- Large marketing-page spacing inside the app.

## IntelligencesTest Adaptation

The in-app billing experience should feel like account management inside enterprise HR software:

- Spanish-first copy.
- Calm dark surfaces.
- Minimal color.
- Current plan first, available plans second.
- Manual billing and PayPal status stated plainly.
- Existing data access always reassured when limits are reached.

## Launch Plans

- Free trial: 3 days, 1 recruiter, 1 project, 10 candidates.
- Starter: 29 EUR/month, 1 recruiter, 1 project, 10 candidates/month.
- Professional: 79 EUR/month, 3 recruiters, 3 projects, 50 candidates/month.
- Enterprise: custom limits and commercial support.
